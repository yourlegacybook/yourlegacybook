Every person can make a difference!


This message and similar ones are commonplace in the world today but how do you actually make a difference? And how do you make a difference that lasts?


Order on Amazon

In Your Legacy, leadership expert David Hodge outlines a blueprint for just that—how you can make a difference that lasts. Hodge walks you through how to intentionally build a life of godly influence to bless others, impact future generations, and ultimately glorify God. Learn from biblical principles of leadership as well as real-life examples and put into practice what you learn with guided exercises and reflection questions.

David Hodge is founder and president of Anchor Leadership, an organization dedicated to encouraging and equipping business and ministry leaders. For over 25 years, David has served as a trainer, consultant, coach, and mentor for both senior-level leaders and rising executives. He lives with his wife and children in Atlanta GA.
